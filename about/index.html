Our Story

A Legacy of Growth and Reliability

Founded in 2002 and based in Derb Omar, Morocco’s largest cosmetics wholesale hub, Boussafa S.A.R.L has grown into a leading distributor of international beauty brands. Over the past 23 years, we have built a reputation for reliability, professionalism, and strong market expertise.

With 100+ employees, 3 warehouses, and over 100 containers imported annually, we ensure seamless logistics, quality control, and nationwide distribution. Our mission is simple: connect global beauty brands with Morocco’s fast-growing retail market through trust and performance.
